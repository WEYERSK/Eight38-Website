The problem

Too Many Options, Not Enough Clarity

You've decided to automate. Good. But now comes the hard part: choosing the right platform. N8N, Make.com (formerly Integromat), Zapier — they all promise to save you time. They all have impressive feature lists. And they all have very different pricing models that make comparison nearly impossible.

Pick the wrong platform and you'll spend months building workflows that hit limitations you didn't see coming. You'll pay for features you don't need, or worse, discover your platform can't handle what you actually need it to do. Migration between platforms is expensive and time-consuming.

The reviews online are mostly written by affiliates pushing whichever platform pays the highest commission. What you need is an honest, side-by-side comparison built around real-world use cases — not marketing buzzwords.
