About us

The People Behind Your Automation

We build custom AI automation systems that give businesses their time back. Based in Cape Town, working with clients worldwide.

Our story

Built by a Developer Who Got Tired of Watching Businesses Waste Time

Eight38 started with a simple observation: businesses everywhere were spending hours on work that machines should handle. Lead follow-ups sitting in inboxes for days. Reports being copy-pasted together every Monday morning. Content ideas dying in notebooks because there was never enough time.

As a software developer based in Cape Town, I'd spent years building systems that solve problems. But the biggest problem I kept seeing wasn't a lack of software — it was a lack of the right automation. Businesses had the tools. They just didn't have the systems connecting them.

So I started building custom AI automation using N8N for workflow orchestration and Claude AI for the intelligence layer. No cookie-cutter templates. No one-size-fits-all solutions. Every system is designed around how your business actually works.

The approach is people-first. Automation should empower your team, not replace them. It handles the repetitive, time-consuming tasks so your people can focus on the creative, strategic, and relationship-building work that actually moves the needle.
