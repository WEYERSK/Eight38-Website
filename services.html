How it works

Our 4-Step Process for Implementing Workflow Automation

Designed to minimise disruption while maximising value. Most projects go live within 2-4 weeks.

Discovery

We start by understanding your business, not selling you software. We map your current workflows, identify pain points, and pinpoint your top automation opportunities based on time savings and ROI. No pressure — just honest guidance about whether workflow automation makes sense for your business right now.

Design

Once you decide to move forward, we design the automated workflow logic. We map the complete workflow from trigger to completion, define success metrics, and get your approval before we build anything. You'll see exactly what the automation will do and how it will work.

Build & Test

Custom implementation using tools like N8N and Claude AI — whatever fits your needs best. We integrate with your existing tools and systems, test thoroughly, and train your team so they're comfortable with the new automated workflows. You don't need to be technical.

Launch & Support

Workflow automation isn't "set it and forget it." We provide continuous monitoring, optimisation as your business processes change, troubleshooting and adjustments as needed, and scaling to additional workflows when you're ready. You get a partner, not just a vendor.
